FAQ


Where can I get EWT? What exchanges is it listed on?

EWT is currently listed on global exchanges Kraken, KuCoin, Bitmart and Liquid

How can I store my EWT?

Many popular wallets allow you to interact with the Energy Web Chain and store EWT, including MyCrypto, MetaMask, and hardware wallets such as Ledger and Trezor. We have deployed a web-based custom wallet (based on the open-source MyCrypto wallet), which is pre-configured to connect to our Volta test network and the production Energy Web Chain and has been tested with hardware wallets such as Ledger and Trezor.

Is EWT an ERC20 token?

No. The native EWT is not an ERC20 token. However, we have developed the EWT Bridged (EWTB) token, which is an ERC20 token that can be minted through a bridge, offering a method for transferring EWT from the Energy Web Chain to the Ethereum mainnet quickly and cost-effectively.
